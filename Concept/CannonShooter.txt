Cannon Shooter

We are building a cannon shooting game. Each player has a movable base, and it has HP 100.
This is turn-based game. If one of base's HP becomes 0, then the other player wins the game.
The player can choose a ball type.
The base can launch a cannonball. If the ball hits an opponent, the HP will decrease based on the ball type.
Ball has various types.
black: basic, if it hits, then damages 5HP.
red: more damage - if it hits, then damages 15HP
yellow: more speed - this ball is the fastest ball
blue: this ball guarantees that this will always hit the opponent
purple: steal HP - if this hits then it steals 10HP from opponent
